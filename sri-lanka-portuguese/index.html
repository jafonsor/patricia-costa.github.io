Sri Lanka Portuguese
ISO 639-3: idb
Sri Lanka Portuguese is an endangered Portuguese-based creole language spoken by c. 1300 speakers scattered across the Jaffna, Trincomalee, Batticaloa, and Ampara districts of Sri Lanka (Cardoso et al. 2019).
