Sri Lanka Portuguese
ISO 639-3: idb

Sri Lanka Portuguese is an endangered Portuguese-based creole spoken as L1/L2 by the Portuguese Burghers, a Eurasian community claiming Portuguese ancestry. Currently, the language is spoken by c. 1300 speakers scattered across the Jaffna, Trincomalee, Batticaloa, and Ampara districts of Sri Lanka (Cardoso et al. 2019).
