University of Lisbon • CLUL

I am a PhD candidate in Linguistics at University of Lisbon. My primary research interest is language variation and change, especially in contexts of language contact, obsolescence and endangerment.

I primarily draw on empirical insights from Sri Lanka Portuguese, an endangered Portuguese-based creole spoken by c. 1300 speakers in the Northern and Eastern provinces of Sri Lanka.
