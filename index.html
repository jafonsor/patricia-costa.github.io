Patrícia Costa
University of Lisbon • CLUL

I am a PhD researcher in Linguistics at the University of Lisbon's Center for Linguistics (CLUL). My research focuses on language variation in creole speech communities characterized by language contact, obsolescence and endangerment.

I primarily draw on empirical insights from Sri Lanka Portuguese, an endangered language spoken by c. 1300 speakers in the Northern and Eastern provinces of Sri Lanka.

I also teach Portuguese as a Foreign Language at the Institute of Portuguese Language and Culture (ICLP).

Find out more about my research or see my CV and Ciência Vitae.

Outside of work, you can find me reading, designing stuff or learning தமிழ்.
