Patrícia Costa
University of Lisbon • CLUL

I am a PhD candidate in Linguistics at the University of Lisbon's Center for Linguistics (CLUL). My primary research interest is language variation and change, especially in contexts of language contact, obsolescence and endangerment.

I primarily draw on empirical insights from Sri Lanka Portuguese, an endangered Portuguese-based creole spoken by c. 1300 speakers in the Northern and Eastern provinces of Sri Lanka.
